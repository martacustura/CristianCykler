Specifications

The Speedster is a handcrafted urban bicycle featuring the Gates Carbon belt drive system. The standard model is equipped with the CDN belt drive system and a 2 speed kick shift Sturmey Archer hub making it light, fast and durable. It features an pursuit/track style frame meant to go fast in the urban environment.

For the basic version of this frame we use both silver brazing and TIG welding to join the tubes. The bottom bracket, monostay and dropouts are cast lugs, the tubes are Dedacciai Zero complete set. We do offer a complete lug set for this frame.
